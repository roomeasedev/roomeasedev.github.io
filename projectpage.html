RoomEase

Living together has never been easier

Overview

RoomEase is a roommate app that makes communicating with roommates and managing your household a lot easier. It provides five main features: reserving household items or places at certain times, writing shared lists among roommates, managing fridge content, scheduling chores, and a reminders feed.

Instructions

Obtaining and installing our app is as easy as one, two, three:

  1. Enable “unknown sources” on your phone.
    • Android is defaulted to forbid installing apps downloaded from outside of Google Play Store. To change that go to Settings > Security, then check the box right next to “Unknown Sources.” Your phone now will allow apps to be installed from sources other than the Play Store.

Download

Download the latest APKs to install and use on your android device. Note, you must enable the installation of apps from unknown sources on your device in order to download and use this apk. Currently, you can only obtain RoomEase on Android 4.0 or higher.
